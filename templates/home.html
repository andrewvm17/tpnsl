Who can play?

We have divisions for all players - from 3 year olds to end of High School. Children as young as three years old can join if they are ready to kick a ball and play as part of a group. In general, nursery schoolers should sign up for the "Pre-K" Division, kindergarteners for the "K" Division, first-graders for Division One, and so on. First-grade girls and older have the option of choosing "all-girls" or "coed."

In accordance with Montgomery County guidelines for COVID-19, masks are optional for players and spectators, but we encourage adults to wear masks if they are not vaccinated. If anyone in your household is experiencing symptoms of COVID-19 please inform your coach or a league administrator and remain home. Players who cannot attend school because of Covid protocols also are not allowed to attend soccer practices or games. We will update these guidelines as necessary.
